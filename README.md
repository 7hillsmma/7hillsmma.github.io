    <h2>👊 About Us</h2>
    <p>We are a competition-focused team dedicated to practical self-defense and sport MMA. Whether you're looking to get in shape or step into the cage, we provide a safe but hard-working environment. </p>
    <p><strong>Coach Ron King III:</strong> Ron King III brings 17 years of martial arts experience, 30 muay Thai fights from the age of 12 to 23 years old including in England, Thailand and Italy to this program. Ron also has a background in wrestling that started at the age of 14 and a submission grappling background that he began to develop in 2019 to improve his striking skills. Born in Washington, DC in 1998 and now living in Richmond, Virginia. At 17 years old, Ron was already two-time WKA National Champion, a double silver medalist at the WKA World Tournament, and an active junior competitor at a time when youth muay Thai was at its least developed compared to today. After 18 fights by the time he was 17 years old, he began to adults in the A-class amateur division due to a lack of fights in his weight class. This also signals the same time period in which he began coaching at the Yamasaki Academy of Woodbridge in Woodbridge, VA and running the muay Thai program there from 2018-2021. In order to pursue his history degree after graduating from Northern Virginia Community College, Ron moved to Richmond in 2021 to focus on accomplishing his main goal of getting his bachelor's degree which he did as of 2024 while running his first personal training business: Black Diamond Personal Training, LLC. After deciding to focus on his true passion, Seven Hills MMA turned from an idea he had in his personal training days into a reality starting in March 2025. Now he seeks to develop Seven Hills MMA into a program that is rooted within the community to bring the skills he has learned internationally back to the neighborhood - his only goal he has wanted to accomplish ever since he started his journey as a coach about a decade ago. </p>
